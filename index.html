Order Process

A complete build can take up to 6 buisness days to complete. We offer an option of clients sending us desired components for the build, or you can purchase parts for your custom mechanical keyboard from our webstore.

Fill order form
Complete the order form with your desired options and someone will send you a email to get started with the build process.
Choose components
Send us your desired components for the keyboard or purchase them from our webstore
Keyboard build
Your custom mechanical keyboard will be built by someone who's been in this hobby for years and has done hundreds of commisioned builds.
Finish
Once the build is finished we wil carefully pack your keyboard and ship it back to you within 6 buisness days from your order date.
Arrives
